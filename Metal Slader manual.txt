Message:
What to do if the screen flickers?
Metal Slader Glory makes the screen flicker at specific moments in order to
render its high-level animations. However, this should not happen haphazardly.
For cases like these, a timing adjust screen has been set up before the
beginning of the game.
(Photo) If you press Select at the copyright screen right after turning on the
console, this screen will appear.
If red flickering marks appear at both sides of this screen, a readjustment
is required. Press the Select button until the red marks disappear. There
are four levels of adjustment, so the color of the screen will change every
time you press Select.
If the red marks don't disappear after pressing Select a certain number of
times, a message telling you to cut off the power and restart the adjustment
will appear.
If you press the A button after finishing with the adjustments, a message
informing you about in-game adjustments will appear. If you then press
A again, the game will start.
If the screen flickers in-game, press Start while holding Select until the
screen stabilizes.

Save your game using a straightforward password system
Metal Slader Glory uses a "Save & Continue" system where
you receive a password at specific points of the game's plot.
Once you have advanced a certain amount, you'll be given the
option to rest, often by Elina who will ask you if you want to do it.
If you choose to rest, you'll see a password to access to the point
of the game you're currently in. If you want to stop playing in the
middle of the game, first write down Elina's words (the password
itself), and then turn off the console.

at the title screen, and after inputting the password, the game will
start.

メッセジー：
いつの話？
メタルスレイダーグロリーは、皆さんが住んでいる時代から数えて約７０年後、西暦２０６２年の物語である。あの忌まわしき、長期にわたる戦争も８年前に終り、地球は平和そのもの。そんな時代背景だ。しかし、忠は知ってしまった。なんでも、地球は危機に瀕していると言う事を…。ところで、この物語の季節は、夏。というわけで、小学生のあずさは夏休み。だから学校に行かないで、宇宙への冒険に動向したのでした。
人々は宇宙へ
この頃になると、人々が宇宙に行くのは当然の事になっていた。（もっとも小学生のあずさは、宇宙に行くの、初めてぇと無邪気に喜んでいたが…）月には数数の娯楽、観光施設が広がり、宇宙空間にも人々が生活できる円筒形の居住区があちらこちらにあった。こちらと地球と結ぶ、民間航空のシャトル便も、かなりの数が運航されている。メタルスレイダーグロリーでの宇宙は、皆さんが住んでいる時代の宇宙よりも、ずっとずっと身近いな存在なのである。
当時のエネルギー事情
西暦２０４０年代初頭から、環境保護のため、ディセルエンジン、ガスタービンエンジンなどの、油種類を燃料とする機械は、地球規模での、ターブー（使
ってはいけないもの）とされてきた。その変わりに、太陽エネルギーを利用したソーラーモーターや水素エンジンにたよる事になったが、パワー不足はまぬがれなかった。さらに、重機にメーカー各社は厳しい経営難におちいる。その結果、あらたに月にＩＳＤＣと呼ぶ研究機関を設置。新しい技術開発に取り組んでいた。
メタルスレイダー誕生
そのＩＳＤＣは、ついに新技術の作業機械の開発に成功した。工作ロボットを応用した人間並の腕。２本脚歩行が可能なオペレーションシステム。また衝撃にも強い樹脂加工を施した外板をそなえ、ソーラーパーワーでも十分な運動性能が得られるよう超軽量化した、メタルギアームの登場である。………１０年後………その作業機械は戦闘用となり、さらに進化し、呼び名を変えた。そのなをメタルスレイダーと言う。すなわちメタルスレイダーは、当時の困難なエネルギー事情を科学が克服した好例と言えるだろう。
メタルスレイダーは、それぞれ機重番号やニックネームを持っている。“グローリー”とは、その名の通り、高性能メタルスレイダーの、栄誉ある称号である。

Message:
When does the story happen?
The events of Metal Slader Glory occur in the year 2062, roughly 70 years from the time you currently live in. Earth is the very image of peace, eight years after the end of that long, abominable war. That's the kind of age it is. However, Tadashi accidentally found out the truth, that the earth is now in grave danger...
By the way, the season where this story happens is summer. Meaning, Azusa is on summer holidays. That's why she can travel to space instead of going to school.
People go to space
In this age, it has become completely natural for people to go to space (Even Azusa innocently rejoices as she claims that this is her first time to space...). In the Moon, there are several amusement and touristic facilities, and people can even live in the void of space, thanks to cylindrical "colonies" built everywhere through it. Several shuttles for civil aviation connect these places with Earth. The space in Metal Slader Glory's universe is a lot more familiar of a place than the space you know in your time.
Energy situation
From the beginning of the decade of 2040, for the sake of environmental protection, machines using Diesel engines or gas turbines that use combustible fuel were tabooed across the Earth. Hydraulic and solar engines that take advantage of the Sun's energy were employed in their place. However, this was not enough to supply the energy demands. Even worse, heavy machinery companies fell into serious financial difficulties. As a result, a new research facility called "I.S.D.C." was established in the Moon. They strove to develop new technologies.
Metal Slader's creation
I.S.D.C. succeeded in developing new and modern work machines. Construction robots were now at the reach of the average person. Its operation system supported bipedal motion. It had installed resin treatments for the outer layers that made it impact-proof, as well as ultra-weight saving granting it enough mobility even with Solar Power. It was the age of the Metal Gearm.
Ten years later, that same work machine was employed by the military, and experienced another evolution, as well as a new name, Metal Slader. In other words, the Metal Slader is probably a good case-example of science conquering past energy issues. Every Metal Slader received a code number as well as a nickname. As the name well states, Glory is the honorific title for the most powerful Metal Slader.
