Message:
What to do if the screen flickers?
Sometimes the screen flickers during cutscenes, however, this should not happen during random moments.
Have no fear, a "timing tweak screen" is here to alleviate this issue.

When you power on the console, access this screen by pressing the 'Select' button at the copyright screen.

If you notice flickering red marks, some adjustments will have to be made.
Press the 'Select' button until the red marks disappear

There's four levels of adjustment, so the screen's color will change every time you press 'Select'.

If the red marks don't disappear after pressing 'Select' after so many times, you'll be prompted with a message to restart the console.

If you press the 'A' button after finishing with the adjustments, a message informing you about in-game adjustment feature will appear. If you then press 'A' again, the game will start.

If the screen flickers in-game, press 'Start' while holding 'Select' until the screen stabilizes.

Metal Slader Glory has a straightforward password system to save your progress.

Time of the Story:

Metal Slader Glory's story takes place in 2062, roughly 70 years in the future. After a long war eight years ago, Earth is at peace. People go to space:
------------------
In the future, it's normal for people to go to space -- even Azusa innocently rejoices it's her first time going to space. On the Moon, there's several touristic attractions. In fact, people live in space thanks to cylinder-like structures called "colonies". They're built everywhere in space. Various spaceships connect these colonies to Earth. Metal Slader Glory's universe isn't that much different from yours once you think about it.

Energy situation:
-----------------
Since the 2040s, fossil fuels were banned on Earth to help save the environment, and hydraulic and solar power became commonplace. However, these fuels couldn't meet humanity's energy demands. Even worse, various heavy machinery companies fell into serious financial difficulties. As a result, a new research facility called "I.S.D.C." was established on the Moon to develop new technologies.

Metal Slader's creation:
-----------------------
I.S.D.C. successfully developed new and modern work machinery, one being construction robots. They have two human-like arms and can walk on two legs. Additionally, they're equipped with a shock-resistant resin-coated armor. They're also extremely light which allows them to be solar powered.

Birth of the Metal Slader:
--------------------------
Ten years later, the military developed their own type of robot: the Metal Slader. In other words, the Metal Sladers are a shining example of humanity overcoming the energy crisis. Every Metal Slader received a code number as well as a nickname. Glory, as the name implies, means its most powerful Metal Slader.
